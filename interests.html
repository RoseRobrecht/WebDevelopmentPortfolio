Literally My Favorite Things in the World

Rock Climbing

I have been rock climbing since I was about ten years old. I love rock climbing because it is a very good workout and keeps me fit, and it's also just very fun. There is no feeling more satisfying than sending a hard climb that you have been working at for a while. I sport climb and lead climb both in a gym and outdoors. I also boulder, but I don't like it quite as much as I love sport climbing.

Drawing and Art

Never said I'm good at it, but I love it anyway. My mom is a professional painter; she sells her oil, watercolor and acrylic paintings in various art galleries across Utah. She is where I get my inspiration for drawing and painting. My favorite art forms are drawing cartoon characters and also doing acrylic pour paintings.

Eating Pasta

I don't really know why I love it so much, I just really love pasta. I'm not even picky about the type of pasta. I just love all pastas. Lasagna is probably my favorite pasta, but not by much. And of course if I am eating pasta, there must be garlic bread to go with it (I love that stuff almost as much as I love pasta)

Knitting and Crocheting

It makes me seem older than I am (which could be a bad thing or a good thing, I'm not quite sure), but I love it anyway. I make scarves, blankets, and sweaters, as well as some other random things like stuffed cactuses and mice. Never trust me in a yarn store with a credit card. Last time I was left alone in this situation I left with almost $300 of yarn...

Reading and Writing

As much as I love computer programming, I have never been one to spend copious ammounts of time surfing the web. I much prefer curling up on the coutch with a good book and a nice cup of tea. In addition to reading stories, I write my own stories, mostly short stories although I am currently working on a longer book.
